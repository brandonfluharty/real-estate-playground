Market Comparison: Twenty metros where strategic sellers cluster

National averages are useless for your specific decision. The price-to-rent ratio tells you whether buying or renting is cheaper on a monthly basis in your metro. Above 20 favors renting. Below 15 favors buying. Between 15 and 20, the answer depends on hold period.

Metro Median Home Price Median Rent (Mo) Price-to-Rent Ratio Signal
How to read this: The price-to-rent ratio divides the median home price by the annual rent. A ratio of 20 means the home costs 20 years of rent. Higher ratios mean buying is relatively more expensive. Data is illustrative and based on publicly available metro-level estimates — always verify with local market research before making decisions.
